Overview
What we're building and why
Design and build a Salesforce Community portal for Distributors to submit and manage pricing claims, replacing the current Citrix-based PoP system. Distributors will NOT use Citrix, the bid system, or any other pathway — this portal is their only interface.

4
Validation rules
All rules run at submission time against SAP data
1
Bid number must be valid and active in SAP
2
Distributor must be listed on the bid
3
Product must be on the bid
4
Claim quantity cannot exceed what the Distributor purchased in SAP — system checks SAP billing history (VBRK/VBRP) for Distributor + Product within ±24 months of the Distributor's Invoice Date
5
Bid price and current customer SAP price are pulled and compared
6
Credit calculated using multiple pricing tables — line and invoice discounts applied to determine net invoice price
5
Processing & status flow
From submission through SAP credit memo creation
1
Submission
Distributor submits claim. Batch ID generated immediately and displayed to Distributor. Distributor receives email notification confirming submission.
2
Validation
System runs all validation rules (Section 4). Errors flagged immediately — Distributor sees them in portal. Sandra receives automated email on both success and failure.
3
Pending
Valid claims queue for overnight processing to SAP. Status = Pending.
4
SAP processing (overnight)
Credit memos created in SAP. Limited to 500 lines per memo — large uploads generate multiple memos. Credits under $150 held in Status 900 and combined with future credits. No negative uploads — returns handled as separate rebills.
5
Complete
Distributor receives notifications from both PoP and SAP. SAP provides a credit document that differs from the PoP document.
Business rules
RuleDetail
Credit memo line limit500 lines per memo — large uploads auto-split into multiple memos
Minimum credit thresholdCredits under $150 held in Status 900 · combined with future credits
Negative uploadsNot supported · returns handled as separate rebills
Rebill minimum$150 minimum for rebills — Bill investigating TBD

Acceptance criteria
Definition of done
Distributor can log in to the Community and submit a claim (upload or manual)
Distributor can choose Claims Approval or Not for Credit at submission
Required fields enforced · optional fields available but not required
Batch ID generated immediately on submission and displayed to Distributor
Validation runs against SAP — bid validity, Distributor, product, quantity, pricing
Sandra receives automated email on both success AND failure — no missed failure notifications
Distributor sees errors immediately in portal · log file accessible
Distributor can identify self-serviceable errors vs. Sandra-only errors
Credit memos limited to 500 lines · credits under $150 held in Status 900
Distributor can search claim history — General Search and Credit Memo Criteria
Distributor can only view their own data — no access to SAP data or other Distributors
Admin can provision a new Distributor user
