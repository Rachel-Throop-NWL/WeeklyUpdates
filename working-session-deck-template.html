What we're aligning on today
1
Pull — SAP billing data for claim validation
Distributors submit claims against their SAP purchase history. Salesforce needs to query VBRK/VBRP for Sold-to + Material within ±24 months of the Distributor's Invoice Date to validate each claim.
PULL from SAP
2
Pull — ZCMIR Customer Material # records
Some Distributors use their own product material numbers. These must be cross-referenced to Newell SAP Material numbers via the ZCMIR table — per line item, potentially 40,000 lookups per upload.
PULL from SAP
3
Push — Credit memo creation in SAP
Once a claim is validated, Salesforce must trigger the creation of a credit memo in SAP. This is an SD sales document — not a simple write. Confirmation (memo number, status, date) must return to Salesforce.
PUSH to SAP
Goal of this call: Make sure the right people are aware of these requirements, discuss integration approach and ownership, and determine where to log and track the work.
1

Pull — SAP billing data

Claim validation against VBRK / VBRP

Requirement 1 of 3
What we know
SAP tables
VBRK (billing document header) · VBRP (billing document item)
Query parameters
Sold-to (Distributor ID) + Material + Invoice Date ±24 months
Invoice Date
Distributor's date to their end customer — NOT an SAP date. Used as center of the 24-month lookup window.
Selection criteria
Sales Orgs US07/US72 · Divisions 40–45 · Account Groups Z001/Z011
Current state
Daily batch export from SAP — not real-time. Goal is to move to real-time query.
What we need to confirm

Is real-time query of VBRK/VBRP feasible for claim validation?

What is the recommended integration method — MuleSoft, API, or direct callout?

What is the expected performance/response time for a real-time query at this volume?

If real-time is not feasible — what is the best approach to replicate the daily batch in Salesforce?

2

Pull — ZCMIR records

Customer Material # cross-reference · SFDC-25966

Requirement 2 of 3
What we know
What ZCMIR is
SAP table that cross-references a Distributor's own product material number to the Newell SAP Material #
When it's needed
Some Distributors enter their own Customer Material # on claim line items instead of the Newell SAP Material #
Scope
Per line item lookup — up to 40,000 lookups on a large upload file
Current gap
ZCMIR records exist in SAP but do NOT currently exist in Salesforce — SFDC-25966 open
What we need to confirm

How should ZCMIR records be brought into Salesforce — real-time lookup, scheduled sync, or manual load?

What is the volume and frequency of ZCMIR record changes in SAP?

What is the recommended approach to support 40,000 per-line lookups at upload time?

Who owns the ZCMIR table in SAP and who can provide the records?

Confirm during discussion
3

Push — Credit memo creation

SAP SD sales document · triggered from Salesforce

Requirement 3 of 3
What we know
What it is
Credit memo is an SAP SD sales document — not a simple data write. Requires document type, sales org, pricing conditions, and line items.
Business rules
Limited to 500 lines per memo · large uploads generate multiple memos · credits under $150 held in Status 900
Confirmation needed
SAP must return credit memo number, status, and date back to Salesforce so the Distributor can see settlement details in the portal
Current state
Runs overnight in the homegrown system — processed as a batch to SAP
What we need to confirm

What SAP SD document type should be used for the credit memo?

What data payload does SAP need to create the credit memo?

What is the recommended push mechanism — MuleSoft, BAPI, RFC, or other?

How does SAP confirm back to Salesforce — synchronous response or async callback?
